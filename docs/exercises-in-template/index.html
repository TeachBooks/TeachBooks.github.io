Exercises in Template

By Tom van Woudenberg / 12/13/2024

We added exercises to our template! With these exercises, we try to get people started a bit more after taking our template to create their first book.

The exercises focus on the key components of a book: the content, the table of contents and the configuration file. Furthermore, it shows you the potential of git in the book-editing process and how the TeachBooks deploy-book-workflow takes care of all the parsing stuff in the background.

We plan on adding much more exercises, so feel free to contribute!
